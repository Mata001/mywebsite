ABOUT ME

I’m a designer & art director based in Los Angeles specializing in visual brand identities & digital design.

His work has been recognised by One Show, ADC, Webby Awards, and Cannes Lions. When Jonny isn't busy educating people on the difference between fonts and typefaces, he can be found taking part in competitive tickling competitions across the UK.
